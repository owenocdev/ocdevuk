Meet Your Instructor

Hello, I’m Susan. My journey is rooted in helping individuals rediscover internal balance, inner peace, and physical vitality through the profound combination of mindful movement and acoustic therapy.

As a fully qualified yoga instructor with advanced credentials in acoustic immersion therapy, I carefully curate environments that blend traditional physical postures with therapeutic frequencies. What is Sound Yoga?

Mindful Movement

Gentle, structural alignment flows designed to deliberately unblock tension, lengthen tight muscular fibers, and physically prepare your body for immersive stillness.

Vibrational Harmony

Live, organic soundscapes targeting your sensory systems. These therapeutic resonance tools naturally soothe the nervous system and help lower sustained stress states.

Extended Savasana

Every class culminates in a prolonged, immersive sound bath relaxation. Rest completely while waves of soothing frequencies carry you into effortless cellular recovery.
