Contrast Therapy

Where the heat meets the chill.

Ice & Embers is a traditional, wood-fired sauna experience designed to pull you away from the noise of daily life. Rooted in Nordic wellness traditions, we pair the intense, comforting heat of a real timber sauna with the grounding clarity of cold exposure.

Whether you are joining our community public slots to connect with like-minded souls or booking out the entire space for a private escape, you'll leave feeling completely recalibrated.

Why We Sauna

The profound physical and mental benefits of regular thermal contrast therapy.

🫀

Cardiovascular Health

The deep heat mimics moderate exercise, improving circulation, blood flow, and overall heart function.

🧘

Deep Nervous Reset

Shifting from hot to cold forces your mind into the absolute present, drastically lowering cortisol and stress levels.

💪

Muscle Recovery

Perfect for athletes. Flushes out metabolic waste and relaxes tight joints and muscles instantly.
