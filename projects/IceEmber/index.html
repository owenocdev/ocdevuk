About Us

Where the heat meets the chill.

Ice & Embers is a traditional, wood-fired sauna experience designed to pull you away from the noise of daily life. Rooted in Nordic wellness traditions, we pair the intense, comforting heat of a real timber sauna with the grounding clarity of cold exposure.

Whether you are joining our community public slots to connect with like-minded souls or booking out the entire space for a private escape, you'll leave feeling completely recalibrated.

Frequently Asked Questions

When should I arrive? ▼

Please arrive 5–10 minutes before your session to allow time for parking and walking to our off-grid sauna location.

There is a short walk from the car park through the top field to the sauna and cold plunge area. We recommend arriving ready in your swimwear and/or dry robe, as we do not currently have changing facilities on site.

Unfortunately, if you arrive late, we are unlikely to be able to extend your session, so please ensure you arrive in plenty of time and ready to enjoy your experience.

What should I bring? ▼

Please bring:

  • Suitable swimwear (avoid metal fastenings, as these may become hot when heated)
  • Two towels – one to sit on in the sauna for hygiene and one to dry off afterwards
  • Flip-flops, Crocs or suitable footwear to wear between the sauna and cold plunge area
  • Water bottle – we provide complimentary fruit-infused water, but it is important to stay hydrated
  • Warm clothing for after your session

Please note that we do not have showers or running water on site.

For your safety, please do not take glass bottles or metal water bottles into the sauna.

We provide a sheltered area for personal belongings and hooks for towels. Please keep belongings to a minimum and avoid bringing valuables, as all items are left at your own risk.

How long are sauna and cold plunge sessions? ▼

Our standard sessions last 55 minutes.

We recommend limiting sauna exposure to a maximum of 20 minutes at a time. If sauna bathing is new to you, start with shorter sessions of 3–5 minutes and gradually increase the duration as you become more comfortable.

Take regular breaks to cool down, spend time in the fresh air, use the cold plunges, or cool off using the buckets provided.

Please use the watering cans to rinse off before entering the cold plunges.

Most importantly, listen to your body and participate at a pace that feels comfortable for you.

Will I be sharing the sauna space? ▼

This depends on the type of session you book.

Community Sessions
Our community sessions are shared experiences with up to 6 guests using the sauna and cold plunge area.

Private Sessions
Private sessions are available if you would prefer exclusive use of the space for yourself, friends, family or a group. Private bookings can accommodate up to 8 guests.

What are your age restrictions? ▼

Unfortunately, due to our insurance requirements, we are unable to admit anyone under 16 years of age.

What facilities are available on site? ▼

As you arrive at the car park, you will pass Denhead Farm Shop, where you will find:

  • Toilet facilities
  • Hot drinks
  • Local produce and refreshments

Please note that Ice & Embers currently does not have changing rooms, showers or running water on site.

Will you be open all year round? ▼

Yes.

At Ice & Embers, we embrace every season. Whether it’s sunshine, rain, frost or crisp winter mornings, the sauna experience remains just as enjoyable and beneficial.

In the unlikely event of severe weather conditions, such as high winds or thunderstorms, we may need to cancel or reschedule sessions for safety reasons. If this happens, we will contact you as soon as possible.

Is the cold plunge mandatory? ▼

No. The cold plunge is completely optional.

Many guests enjoy alternating between the sauna and cold water, while others simply relax in the sauna and fresh air. You are welcome to participate in whichever elements of the experience feel right for you.

Can I visit if I have never used a sauna before? ▼

Absolutely.

Our sessions are suitable for both beginners and experienced sauna users. We encourage first-time guests to take things slowly, stay hydrated and listen to their bodies throughout the session.

What is your cancellation policy? ▼

We understand that plans can change, and we will always do our best to be fair and flexible.

Community Sessions

  • Cancellations made more than 48 hours before the session start time are eligible for a full refund or transfer to another available session.
  • Cancellations made within 48 hours of the session start time are non-refundable.

Private Sessions

  • Cancellations made more than 7 days before the booking are eligible for a full refund or transfer to another available date.
  • Cancellations made within 7 days of the booking are non-refundable.

Rescheduling
Where possible, we may allow bookings to be transferred to another date at our discretion. Requests should be made as early as possible.

No-Shows and Late Arrivals
Guests who do not attend their booking, or who arrive too late to participate, will not be eligible for a refund or transfer.

Severe Weather
In the unlikely event that Ice & Embers needs to cancel a session due to severe weather or unforeseen circumstances, guests will be offered either a full refund or the opportunity to transfer their booking to another available session.
