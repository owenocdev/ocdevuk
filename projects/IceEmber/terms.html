This Agreement sets out the terms and conditions governing the use of the sauna, cold plunge and associated facilities at Wester Denhead Farm, Coupar Angus ("the Facility").

By booking, attending, participating in, or using the Facility, participants confirm that they have read, understood and agreed to the terms contained within this Agreement.

Health Declaration

I confirm that:

  • I am aged 16 years or over.
  • I am physically and medically fit to participate in sauna bathing, cold-water immersion and contrast therapy activities.
  • I am not under the influence of alcohol or drugs.
  • I do not have any illness, infection, open wound or contagious condition that could place myself or others at risk.
  • I understand that sauna use and cold-water immersion may not be suitable for individuals with certain medical conditions including, but not limited to, cardiovascular conditions, high blood pressure, epilepsy, respiratory conditions or other health concerns.

I further acknowledge that if I have any existing medical condition, am taking prescription medication, have recently undergone surgery or medical treatment, am pregnant or believe I may be pregnant, or have any doubt whatsoever regarding my suitability to participate, I must consult my GP or another suitably qualified healthcare professional before using the Facility.

I understand that the Facility does not provide medical advice and that participation is entirely at my own risk.

Acknowledgement of Risk

I understand and accept that participation in sauna bathing, cold-water immersion, contrast therapy and associated activities carries inherent risks including but not limited to:

  • Heat exhaustion
  • Heat stroke
  • Dehydration
  • Burns from hot surfaces
  • Dizziness or fainting
  • Adverse reactions to cold-water immersion
  • Slips, trips and falls
  • Aggravation of existing medical conditions
  • Serious injury or death in rare circumstances

I voluntarily choose to participate despite these risks. I accept responsibility for monitoring my own physical condition throughout my visit and agree to leave the sauna or cold plunge immediately if I feel unwell, dizzy, faint, nauseous or otherwise uncomfortable.

Conditions of Use

To ensure the safety and enjoyment of all users, I agree to:

  • Follow all instructions given by staff
  • Respect other participants and staff at all times
  • Wear appropriate swimwear
  • Use all facilities responsibly
  • Enter and exit the sauna and cold plunge facilities with care
  • Report any accident, injury, hazard or unsafe condition immediately

I understand that I must not:

  • Run within the facility area
  • Engage in unsafe or reckless behaviour
  • Tamper with equipment
  • Bring glass containers into the sauna area
  • Use the facilities whilst intoxicated
  • Enter restricted areas
