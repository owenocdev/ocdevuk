How I Work

My Simple Process

01
Step 01
Discovery & Wireframe

We talk through what you need, who it's for, and what you want it to feel like. I'll then sketch out a wireframe so we're aligned before anything is designed or built.

02
Step 02
First Draft Design

I'll put together a first full design in Figma for your review. You tell me what you love, what to tweak, and we refine it together until it's right.

03
Step 03
Refinement & Launch

Once the design is approved I build it out, test it across devices, and hand it over — with everything set up and ready for you to use from day one.
